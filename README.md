### What I'm Building

**Hermes → JARVIS**
AI agent evolving into a full JARVIS-level assistant. Voice commands, smart home control, cross-device memory, camera vision. Running on WSL with multi-provider LLM backend.

**BeneathArcantum** 🏆
3D platformer that placed **4th in state** at SkillsUSA. 53 C# scripts, 12 Unity scenes — built by a high school team at Hourhand Studios.

**Jenkins Digital Design**
STEAM education YouTube channel in the works. Music, computer engineering, astronomy, digital art, cybersecurity — each contributor teaches their passion.
