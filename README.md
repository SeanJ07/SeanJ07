## 🧠 What I'm Building

I'm building **[Spiral](https://github.com/SeanJ07/second-brain)** — an autonomous AI agent that runs on my hardware. It's not a chatbot. It's a system.

> *"The days of manually searching for flights, scholarships, and transcripts are over. Spiral watches. Spiral works. I just get the report."*

Spiral has its own memory, runs 11 cron jobs, controls my PC, maintains a knowledge vault, and connects through Discord. Think JARVIS — but built by a CS student in his bedroom.
